üìö Deep Dive: What is Regime-Aware Trading?

Most strategies have a fatal flaw: They assume markets behave the same way all the time.

They don't.

Think of markets like weather. Your strategy is your clothing choice.

  • Sunny day (R2 - Trending): Breakouts work. Mean reversion fails. Wear shorts.
  • Rainy day (R1 - Chop): Breakouts fail. Mean reversion works. Bring umbrella.
  • Hurricane (R3 - Extreme): Nothing works reliably. Stay inside.

Static strategies = wearing shorts in a hurricane.

How Sentinel Classifies Regimes:

  1. Wave Energy Analysis (amplitude √ó frequency)
  2. Fractal Structure (swing point density)
  3. Volatility Profile (ATR normalization)
  4. Volume Distribution (order flow imbalance)

Output: R0 (balanced), R1 (chop), R2 (trend), R3 (extreme)

Why This Matters:

Your RSI < 30 "buy signal" works in R2 (trending).
It LOSES in R1 (chop) because price whipsaws through oversold.

Regime-aware strategy: Only take RSI signals in R2. Switch to mean reversion in R1. Reduce size in R3.

Result: Your strategy ADAPTS instead of BREAKS.

This isn't rocket science. It's survival.
